About Motors

Harley-Davidson is an iconic American motorcycle manufacturer founded in 1903 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. It is one of the most well-known motorcycle brands globally and has a passionate following of riders known as "Harley enthusiasts" or "Harley riders."Harley-Davidson motorcycles are known for their distinctive V-twin engines, which produce a unique sound and deliver ample low-end torque. The company offers a range of engine sizes, from the smaller 500cc and 750cc engines to larger 1,200cc and 1,800cc engines.Harley-Davidson offers a diverse range of motorcycles designed for various riding preferences and styles. Some popular models include the Harley-Davidson Street Glide, Road Glide, Fat Boy, Sportster, Softail, and Electra Glide.
